Privacy Policy

At Blood Donor App, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. We do not collect any sensitive medical information or financial data.

Information We Collect:

  • Basic Personal Information: This includes your name, date of birth, blood group, and phone number. We only collect the information you provide directly to us.

How We Use Your Information:

  • Connecting Donors and Recipients: We use your provided information solely to facilitate blood donations by connecting donors with those in need. We do not share this data with third parties.

Data Security:

  • We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Details of these security measures are confidential for security reasons.

Your Rights:

  • You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. Please contact us to exercise these rights.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at blooddonorassistance@gmail.com.

Feel free to contact us for any query.
